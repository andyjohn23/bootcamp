MUSIC

vocal or instrumental sounds (or both) combined in such a way as to produce
beauty of form, harmony, and expression of emotion.

GENRE

A music genre is a conventional category that identifies some pieces of music as
belonging to a shared tradition or set of conventions. Genre is to be distinguished
from musical form and musical style, although in practice these terms are sometimes
used interchangeably.

  • classical music
  • pop
  • reggae
  • Jazz
